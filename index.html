Five steps from signup to keys in hand

1

Register your preferences

Complete our 90-second preference wizard: budget, commute start point, neighbourhood priority, apartment size, and safety tolerance. No lengthy forms—just your must-haves. Sakna saves your profile and begins matching instantly.

2

Receive curated listings

Every morning at 7 AM, Sakna delivers 5–7 apartments scored by nine localised factors (safety, commute time, price, fibre, schools, pharmacies, seismic risk, landlord verification, tenant reviews). Instant alerts fire when new apartments match your exact criteria.

3

Message verified landlords

Every landlord and tenant is verified against national ID and rental history. Message with confidence—scammers are already filtered out. Sakna's in-app messenger keeps all communication secure and documented.

4

Secure deposit via escrow

Skip cash handshakes entirely. Pay your arboon through Dinari escrow—legally protected, digitally verified, fully refundable until you confirm occupancy. Both landlord and tenant see the transaction, eliminating the vanishing-landlord nightmare.

5

Move in. Confirm occupancy.

Visit the apartment, verify condition and landlord identity one final time, then confirm occupancy in Sakna. Escrow releases, landlord receives funds, you have a 100% documented rental agreement. Rental closed in 3–5 days, not weeks.
