How Sakna finds you apartments in 4 steps

1

Register and set preferences

Sign up in under 3 minutes and answer nine questions: commute destination, budget, neighbourhood safety level, fibre requirement, and lifestyle preferences. Your profile becomes your apartment filter. This takes less time than one Facebook group scroll.

2

Sakna hunts across all platforms

Our AI continuously scrapes Ouedkniss, Beytic, Darrna, Facebook, and private landlord networks 24/7. Every listing is scored against your profile, location data, and safety zones in real-time. You never miss an apartment again.

3

Receive curated digests instantly

Wake to a morning digest of 3–5 apartments ranked by fit, or get instant alerts when a new match appears. Click any listing to see commute time, safety score, neighbourhood infrastructure, and verified landlord details. No guesswork.
